# AI Alerts

AI Alerts is a notification service that keeps you updated on information you frequently look for on the internet. Whether it's train disruptions, stock prices, weather updates, or any other query, AI Alerts has you covered. Simply provide your query, WhatsApp number, and desired notification frequency, and receive timely updates directly on WhatsApp.

## Features

- **User-Friendly Interface**: Easily submit queries and manage your notifications.
- **Customizable Notifications**: Set how often you want to be notified about specific information.
- **Real-Time Updates**: Get the latest information summarized and sent directly to your WhatsApp.
- **Versatile Queries**: Monitor anything from public transport disruptions to weather updates and more.

## How It Works

1. **Submit Query**: Enter the information you want to track. For example, "Trains between West London and North London, and let me know if there are any problems."
2. **Provide WhatsApp Number**: Enter the WhatsApp number where you wish to receive notifications.
3. **Set Frequency**: Choose how often you want to receive updates (e.g., every hour).
4. **Receive Updates**: AI Alerts uses the Perplexity API to search for the latest information, summarizes it, and sends it to your WhatsApp at the specified frequency.

## Example Use Case

If you're monitoring train disruptions:
- **Query**: "Check for train disruptions between West London and North London."
- **WhatsApp Number**: "+44xxxxxxxxxx"
- **Frequency**: Every 1 hour

AI Alerts will search for any disruptions on the specified train lines every hour, summarize the findings, and send an update to your WhatsApp.
